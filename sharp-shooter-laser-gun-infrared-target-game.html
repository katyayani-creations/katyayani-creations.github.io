Key Features:

  • Infrared Target Shooting: Enhances aiming skills and hand-eye coordination.

  • Lights & Music: Engaging features that provide auditory and visual stimulation.

  • Safe & Durable: Made from non-toxic, sturdy ABS plastic ensuring safety during play.

  • Easy to Use: Lightweight design suitable for children to handle comfortably.

  • Perfect Gift: Ideal for birthdays, holidays, and special occasions.

Product Dimensions:

  • Gun Dimensions: Approximately 24.5 x 15.5 x 5 cm

  • Target Board Dimensions: Approximately 24.5 x 20 x 6.9 cm

  • Weight: Gun - 149 g; Target Board - 220 g

Package Includes:

  • 1 x Infrared Laser Gun

  • 1 x Target Board

  • 1 x Stand for Target Board

How to Play:

  1. Assemble the stand and mount the target board.

  2. Power on the target board (requires 3 x AA 1.5V batteries).

  3. Insert 2 x AA 1.5V batteries into the laser gun.

  4. Aim and shoot at the center of the target board.

  5. Upon a successful hit, the target board will spin and emit lights and music, indicating a successful shot.
