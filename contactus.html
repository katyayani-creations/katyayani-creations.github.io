Frequently Asked Questions 🤔

Do you accept custom orders? ✨

+

Yes! We love creating personalized magical items. Contact us with your vision, and we'll work together to bring it to life. Custom orders typically take 1-2 weeks depending on complexity.

What are your delivery options? 🚚

+

We offer free delivery for orders above ₹1,500. For smaller orders, delivery charges apply based on location. We typically deliver within 3-7 business days.

Can I return or exchange items? 🔄

+

Yes, we have a 7-day return policy for unused items in original packaging. Custom-made items cannot be returned unless there's a defect.

Do you offer bulk discounts? 💰

+

Absolutely! We offer attractive discounts for bulk orders, especially for events, parties, or business purchases. Contact us for a personalized quote.
