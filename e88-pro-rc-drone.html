Full Product Description

Elevate your aerial photography experience with the E88 Pro RC Drone. Designed for both beginners and seasoned pilots, this foldable quadcopter combines advanced features with user-friendly controls.

Key Features:

  • Dual HD Cameras: Equipped with 4K and 1080P wide-angle cameras to capture high-resolution photos and videos from various angles.

  • Foldable Design: Compact and lightweight, making it easy to carry for outdoor adventures and travel.

  • WiFi FPV Transmission: Real-time video streaming to your smartphone via the dedicated app, allowing for immersive first-person viewing.

  • Altitude Hold Mode: Ensures stable flight and clear images by maintaining a consistent altitude.

  • Headless Mode: Simplifies orientation control, making it easier for beginners to navigate.

  • One-Key Return: Conveniently brings the drone back to its takeoff point with a single button press.

  • Gesture Control: Capture photos and videos with simple hand gestures for hands-free operation.

  • 360° Flips & Rolls: Perform impressive aerial maneuvers to enhance your flying experience

Specifications:

  • Frequency: 2.4GHz

  • Channels: 4CH

  • Motor: 816 Coreless Motor

  • Battery: 3.7V 1800mAh Lipo (Included)

  • Transmitter Battery: Requires 3x 1.5 AA batteries (not included)

  • Flying Time: Approximately 10-15 minutes

  • Charging Time: 100 minutes

  • Control Distance: Up to 100 meters

  • Dimensions: Unfolded: 25 x 20 x 5.5 cm; Folded: 12.5 x 8 x 5.5 cm

  • Color Options: Black and White
